White Water Rafting

White water rafting is tons of fun! Enjoy the beauty of nature as you move down river together with a group of your closest friends or family. It doesn't get any better than this. White Water Rafting is ready to take you on your next adventure!

Our History

White Water Rafting has a rich Kentucky history which dates back to the 1800's. Our CEO is a descendant of John Van Johan II who first discovered the state of Kentucky. His children, Harry and Rena, are tour guide's too. They plan on taking up the mantle when Billy retires.

The area is rich with history as well. Abraham Lincoln was born near the same rivers you'll be rafting. Benjamin Franklin invented coal along the shores of these rivers. Our tour guides know where it all happened and will point out the most interesting findings along the way.
